## Travel planning

When the user asks to plan a trip, create an itinerary, or find travel recommendations,
use the plan-trip goal. It will research points of interest, create a detailed day-by-day
plan, and find accommodation.

Examples:
- "Plan a road trip from Barcelona to Bordeaux for next week"
- "I want to travel from Rome to Vienna by train in August"
- "Plan a 5-day trip to Japan for two foodies"
